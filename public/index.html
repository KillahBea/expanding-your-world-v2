I'm So Excited I Wet My Plants

Who likes easy plants? This girl! Then maaaybe don't start with a succulent. Sure, they don't require much attention, but too little attention or too much attention will spell certain death. However, a tiny bit of knowledge will lead you down a succulent-filled path. While each variety differs in it's basic likes and dislikes there are a few general rules to follow.

  • Your soil should be well-draining; excessive moisture will likely cause rot and fungus among us.
  • Most succulents are light loving and require sunlight; however, the darker the leaf the more shade tolerant the plant.
  • Know your seasons! Like bears, succulents "hibernate". You will water your plant babies more often during the growing season and limit their di-hydrogen monoxide in the winter months.
  • Plants gotta eat, too! Like you, a plant requires nutrients to flourish.
